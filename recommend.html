Our Recommendations

Milk and Honey

Author: Rupi Kaur
Genre:Poetry

Milk and Honey is a bestselling collection of poetry that speaks to emotional survival. It Explores themes of love, abuse, loss, and femininity. It's raw and powerful.

A poetic journey of survival and healing.

Additional Reading tips

The Picture of Dorian Gray

Author: Oscar Wilde
Genre: Classic

A haunting story about the cost of indulgence and the loss of innocence. As Dorian Gray's life moves on, he remains young and beautiful as his portrait withers.

A haunting exploration of vanity and morality.
